Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our application ("the App") handles your data. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from our users. All necessary information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our App does not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other personally identifiable information.

3. Local Data Storage

All necessary data used by the App is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not shared with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, please refer to Google's privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy.

You can manage your ad preferences by visiting: Google Ad Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect, store, or process any data from individuals under 18 years of age. If you are under 18, please do not use this application.

If we become aware of any data inadvertently collected from a child under 18, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

Parents or guardians who believe that their child has provided personal data through third-party ads may review and manage their ad settings via Google Ad Settings.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes means you accept the updated terms.
